About Me

Statewide issues start in the ground. Municipal government networks, grassroots clubs, community meetings. I am a reporter and writer focused on who tills the soil. My favorite places in California are Echo Park Lake, the old motels in Desert Hot Springs, Fernwood Tavern in Big Sur, Dana Point Harbor, anywhere in Laguna Beach, West Anaheim, San Francisco, and the route PCH takes through Salinas.
